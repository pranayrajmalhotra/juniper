No. 03 — Programming

A sound for the small hours.

Selectors

Booked for restraint and feel. No headliners. Sets sit between ninety minutes and three hours.

The room

Always small. Never advertised. The capacity matches the venue's late-night seating, not its dancefloor.

The door

Members enter first. Plus-ones are welcome but counted. The list closes when the room is full.

The hour

From eleven, sometimes ten. Until the venue's licence sleeps — and occasionally a little later.
