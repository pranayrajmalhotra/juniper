No. 02 — Common questions

Questions, answered.

How do I redeem an offer at a partner venue?

Open the offer page on your phone before you arrive and tap Redeem Offer. Show the resulting digital pass to the host. Some venues will also ask for a quick membership check via your registered email.

When does my subscription renew?

Membership runs annually from your join date. We'll send a reminder 45 days before expiry. To renew now or upgrade your tier, drop the concierge a line and we'll process it within one business day.

Can I bring guests?

Yes — most member benefits include "1 member + 3 guests" by default. Some signature programming (the Platinum series, certain private events) is members-only. The offer page will tell you the limit per venue.

I haven't received my verification email.

Check spam, then your "Promotions" tab. If still missing after ten minutes, sign up again with the same email — the system will resend. Persistent issue? Email us with the address you used and we'll resolve it manually.

Can a venue refuse a Juniper redemption?

Only in very specific cases — private events, blackout dates clearly noted on the offer, or dress-code mismatches at members' clubs. If you ever feel unfairly refused a benefit, message the concierge with a screenshot and we'll mediate the same day.

How do I propose a new partner venue?

We love this question. Email partnerships@juniper.club with the venue name, your relationship to it (regular, friend of the owner, etc.) and what you think makes it fit our roster. We curate quietly; the average waiting list is six months.
